Redefining Limb Loss:
Disproving Disability

It's time for the nearly 2 million American amputees
to have a voice in the redesign of their bodies.

Performance

The legs we design are meant to withstand hours of intense play, day in and day out.

Data

As we work with those we help, we make data on how people prefer to move and live open to everyone, so that we can all work together to improve upon these prosthetics.

Design

Our designs will be available here as they are developed, all open source.

Safety

Safety is our top priority. All of our products will be tested to ensure quality, and prevent injury.
